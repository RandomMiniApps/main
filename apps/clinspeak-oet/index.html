What ClinSpeak OET does

ClinSpeak OET is an independent OET Speaking preparation app for nurses. It gives candidates a structured way to practise clinical role plays, record answers and review feedback.

The app focuses on preparation and communication practice. It does not claim to provide an official OET score.

Who is ClinSpeak OET for?

ClinSpeak OET is designed for nurses preparing for OET-style speaking practice.

  • International nurses preparing for OET Speaking
  • Nurses who want timed role-play practice
  • Learners who want transcript and audio replay
  • Candidates building confidence before speaking exams
  • Busy healthcare professionals practising in short sessions
  • Students who want structured feedback and example answers

How ClinSpeak OET works

  1. Choose an OET-style role-play scenario.
  2. Use the timed preparation step.
  3. Record your speaking response.
  4. Review transcript, audio replay and feedback.
  5. Practise again with a clearer structure and stronger communication.

Why use ClinSpeak OET?

OET Speaking preparation often needs repeated spoken practice, not just reading model answers. ClinSpeak OET gives nurses a private, repeatable way to rehearse clinical communication.

The app is a preparation tool and works best alongside official OET guidance, teacher feedback and real speaking practice.
