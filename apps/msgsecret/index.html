What MsgSecret does

MsgSecret helps keep the meaning of your messages out of plain form before the text leaves your device. You set up your cipher, encrypt on device and share ciphertext through any app or pasteboard.

Separate plaintext and ciphertext fields make it easier to switch between encoding and decoding without overwriting your last step.

How MsgSecret works

  1. Choose whether to encrypt or decrypt.
  2. Enter your text and cipher settings.
  3. Run the transformation on device.
  4. Copy the result into email, SMS, chat or social apps.
  5. Decode incoming ciphertext with MsgSecret and the same settings.

Why use MsgSecret?

MsgSecret reduces what service providers and platforms can infer from message text alone because they carry ciphertext instead of the original wording.

It is a cipher utility, not a guarantee of military-grade or end-to-end security. Use it for privacy-minded encoding and learning, not for high-risk secrets.
