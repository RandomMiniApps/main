What BoomColor does

BoomColor is a coloring book app that turns outline art into relaxing tap-to-fill sessions on iPhone and iPad. Pick a page from the gallery, choose colors or effects, and paint region by region with undo and redo support.

The app ships with starter pages in the download and expands through Apple-hosted gallery packs organized into ten browse categories. A daily challenge page appears at the top of Explore with progress goals and bonus credits when you complete them.

Who is BoomColor for?

BoomColor is for anyone who wants a calm creative app without complex drawing tools.

  • Adults looking for a relaxing coloring break
  • Parents and kids sharing simple tap-to-fill play
  • Commuters who want short creative sessions
  • Fans of animals, anime, vehicles and themed art packs
  • People who want to color their own photos
  • iPhone and iPad users who prefer portrait or landscape

How BoomColor works

  1. Open Explore and browse categories, difficulty, favorites or search.
  2. Pick a page—or start with today’s daily challenge at the top of the grid.
  3. Choose a color or unlockable fill effect from the editor palette.
  4. Tap regions to fill them; use undo and redo as you refine your artwork.
  5. Save finished pages to Photos or return later—your progress is remembered.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BoomColor?

BoomColor is a coloring book app for iPhone and iPad. It is published on the App Store under the BoomColor name and presents a Coloring Book experience in the app.

How do I color a page?

Open a page in the editor, select a color or effect, and tap inside outline regions. The app fills the tapped area while respecting line boundaries.

How many pages are in the gallery?

The app includes 50 starter pages in the app download plus more than 650 additional hosted gallery pages across category packs, with new packs delivered through Apple Background Assets.

What are daily challenges?

Each day highlights a challenge page with goals for coverage, color use and effects. Progress is shown in the editor and on Explore, and completing all goals earns bonus credits.

Can I color my own pictures?

Yes. You can import photos from your library to use as coloring pages and save finished artwork to Photos when you are done.

Do I need internet for every page?

Starter pages work offline. Hosted gallery packs download on demand when you open them, so an internet connection helps for those pages.

Is BoomColor free?

BoomColor is free to download. Credits, optional unlocks and ads help support access to additional pages and creative effects.

Important notes

BoomColor is a casual creative app for entertainment and relaxation. Gallery artwork is original outline content for coloring practice, not licensed reproductions of third-party brands unless stated in-app.

Photo import and save features use your device photo library with your permission. Parental guidance is recommended for younger children using in-app purchases or ads.
